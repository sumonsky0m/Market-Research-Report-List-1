<p><ul><li>Kappa Carrageenan</li><li>Lota Carrageenan</li><li>Lambda Carrageenan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The food grade carrageenan market is primarily divided into three types: Kappa, Iota, and Lambda carrageenan. Kappa carrageenan is known for its strong gelling properties and is commonly used in dairy products. Iota carrageenan offers a softer gel and is often utilized in desserts and sauces. Lambda carrageenan does not gel but thickens, making it suitable for dairy and meat products. These variants serve diverse applications across the food industry, catering to specific texture and stability requirements.</p></p>
