<p><p>Endoscope reprocessing detergents are essential for maintaining the cleanliness and safety of medical equipment. The market is primarily divided into enzyme detergents and non-enzymatic detergents. Enzyme detergents utilize proteolytic enzymes to break down organic matter, enhancing cleaning efficiency, especially for proteins and biofilms. In contrast, non-enzymatic detergents rely on surfactants and detergents to remove debris without enzymatic action, serving as a more general cleaning solution. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring effective endoscope reprocessing protocols.</p></p>
