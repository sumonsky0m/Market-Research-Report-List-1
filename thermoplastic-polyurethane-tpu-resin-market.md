<p><p>The Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU) resin market is typically categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I TPU is known for its flexibility, abrasion resistance, and superior elasticity, making it ideal for applications in footwear, automotive parts, and medical devices. Type II TPU, on the other hand, offers enhanced mechanical properties and chemical resistance, making it suitable for industrial applications, such as seals, gaskets, and coatings. Both types cater to diverse industries, driven by distinct performance requirements.</p></p>
