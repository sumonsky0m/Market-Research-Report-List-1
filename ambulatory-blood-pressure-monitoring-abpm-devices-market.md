<p><p>Ambulatory Blood Pressure Monitoring (ABPM) devices are categorized into two main types: arm ABPM devices and wrist ABPM devices. Arm ABPM devices are commonly used for their accuracy and reliability, measuring blood pressure at the upper arm, which is the traditional site for such assessments. In contrast, wrist ABPM devices are more portable and user-friendly, allowing for easier monitoring, especially for individuals with mobility issues. Both types serve the primary purpose of providing continuous blood pressure measurements over a 24-hour period.</p></p>
