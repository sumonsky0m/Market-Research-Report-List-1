<p><p>The highly flexible cables market is divided into two main types: double and single. Single-core highly flexible cables consist of one conductor, ideal for applications requiring simplicity and minimal weight, such as portable equipment. In contrast, double-core cables feature two conductors, providing improved power delivery and redundancy, making them suitable for more complex machinery and automation systems. Both types emphasize enhanced flexibility and durability, catering to industries like manufacturing, robotics, and telecommunications where movement and reliability are critical.</p></p>
