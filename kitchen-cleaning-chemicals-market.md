<p><p>The kitchen cleaning chemicals market is primarily divided into two types: alkali cleaning chemicals and strong acid cleaning chemicals. Alkali cleaning chemicals, often containing compounds like sodium hydroxide, effectively neutralize grease and grime, making them ideal for degreasing surfaces in kitchens. On the other hand, strong acid cleaning chemicals, such as hydrochloric acid, are utilized for removing tough stains and mineral deposits. Both types play essential roles in maintaining hygiene and cleanliness in kitchen environments, catering to different cleaning needs.</p></p>
