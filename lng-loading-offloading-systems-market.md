<p><strong>The LNG Loading & Offloading Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CBM (Conventional Buoy Mooring)</li><li>SPM (Single Point Mooring)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LNG loading and offloading systems are crucial for the transportation of liquefied natural gas. The Conventional Buoy Mooring (CBM) system utilizes a buoy anchored to the seabed, allowing ships to moor and transfer LNG efficiently. In contrast, Single Point Mooring (SPM) systems employ a flexible turret or buoy that enables rotational movement, accommodating diverse weather conditions. Both systems enhance the safety and efficiency of LNG operations, catering to the growing global demand for natural gas.</p></p>
