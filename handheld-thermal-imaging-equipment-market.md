<p><p>The handheld thermal imaging equipment market is primarily divided into two types: infrared thermal imaging and microwave thermal imaging. Infrared thermal imaging utilizes infrared radiation to detect heat emitted by objects, enabling applications in building inspections, electrical assessments, and medical diagnostics. Microwave thermal imaging, on the other hand, uses microwave signals to detect temperature variations, making it useful for monitoring equipment health and for specific industrial applications. Both technologies offer portable solutions for real-time thermal analysis and diagnostics.</p></p>
