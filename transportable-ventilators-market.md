<p><p>The transportable ventilators market is divided into two main types: invasive and non-invasive ventilation. Invasive ventilation involves the use of tubes inserted into the patient's airway to provide mechanical support, often used in critical care settings. Non-invasive ventilation, on the other hand, employs masks or similar devices to deliver air without direct airway access, making it suitable for patients with less severe respiratory issues. Both types are crucial in emergencies, offering flexibility and portability for varied healthcare needs.</p></p>
