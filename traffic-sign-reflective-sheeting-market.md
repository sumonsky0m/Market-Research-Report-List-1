<p><p>The Traffic Sign Reflective Sheeting market comprises two primary types: Glass Bead Type and Micro Prismatic Type. Glass Bead Type sheeting utilizes tiny glass beads to reflect light, offering good visibility at a lower cost, making it popular for various applications. Conversely, Micro Prismatic Type sheeting employs tiny prisms to enhance light reflection, providing superior brightness and visibility, particularly in low-light conditions. This type is more durable and is increasingly favored for high-traffic areas and safety-critical applications.</p></p>
