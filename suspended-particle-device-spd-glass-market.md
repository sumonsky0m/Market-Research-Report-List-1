<p><p>Suspended Particle Device (SPD) glass is categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I SPD glass offers a higher level of light control and offers a clear transition from transparent to opaque, making it ideal for applications where variable privacy and shading are needed, such as in commercial buildings. Type II SPD glass, while still effective, has a slightly reduced performance in light modulation. It is often used in residential settings and areas requiring less intensive light control.</p></p>
