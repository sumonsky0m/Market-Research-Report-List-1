<p><p>The disposable laparoscopic surgical retriever bag market is segmented into two types: detachable and non-detachable bags. Detachable bags allow the surgeon to separate the bag from the retrieval device after use, facilitating easier specimen handling and extraction. In contrast, non-detachable bags remain connected to the retrieval device throughout the procedure, ensuring a more secure containment of the specimen until removal. Both types are designed to enhance surgical efficiency and minimize contamination during laparoscopic procedures.</p></p>
