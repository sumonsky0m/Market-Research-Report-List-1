<p><strong>The Defibrillator Electrodes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Implanted Defibrillator Electrodes</li><li>External Defibrillator Electrodes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The defibrillator electrodes market is segmented into two main types: implanted and external electrodes. Implanted defibrillator electrodes are embedded within the body, typically used in implantable cardioverter-defibrillators (ICDs) to monitor heart rhythms and deliver shocks as needed. In contrast, external defibrillator electrodes are used in external devices, providing immediate electrical shock in emergency situations. Both types are crucial for managing arrhythmias and ensuring effective cardiac resuscitation, serving distinct roles in patient care.</p></p>
