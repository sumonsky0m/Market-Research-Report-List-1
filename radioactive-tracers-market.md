<p><p>The radioactive tracers market is primarily divided into two types: Positron Emission Tomography (PET) and Single Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT). PET uses radionuclides to detect metabolic processes in tissues, offering high-resolution images for early disease detection, particularly in oncology. SPECT, on the other hand, provides functional information about blood flow and cellular activity, commonly employed in cardiology and neurology. Both modalities serve crucial roles in diagnostics, enhancing patient outcomes through effective disease monitoring and management.</p></p>
