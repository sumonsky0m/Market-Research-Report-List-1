The market for QCLs is primarily divided into two types: Pulsed Quantum Cascade Lasers, which are used for applications requiring high peak power and short burst emissions, and Continuous Wave (CW) Quantum Cascade Lasers, which provide a steady output and are ideal for applications needing constant illumination, such as spectroscopy and sensing in various industries.</p></p>
