<p><p>The In-Vitro Diagnostic (IVD) market comprises two primary segments: test equipment and test reagents. Test equipment includes devices and instruments used to perform diagnostic tests, such as analyzers, microscopes, and automated systems. These tools facilitate the detection and analysis of various biological samples. Test reagents, on the other hand, consist of chemical substances and biological materials needed to carry out these tests, such as antibodies, enzymes, and dyes. Together, they enable accurate disease diagnosis and patient monitoring.</p></p>
