<p><p>The unmanned VTOL (Vertical Take-Off and Landing) craft market can be divided primarily into two types: fixed-wing and rotorcraft. Fixed-wing VTOLs combine the efficiency of traditional airplanes with vertical take-off capabilities, making them suitable for longer-range missions and higher speeds. In contrast, rotorcraft VTOLs utilize rotating blades, allowing for enhanced maneuverability and the ability to operate in confined spaces. Each type serves distinct applications, ranging from surveillance and cargo transport to emergency response and urban air mobility.</p></p>
