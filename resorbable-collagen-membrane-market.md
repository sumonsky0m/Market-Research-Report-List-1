<p><p>The resorbable collagen membrane market is primarily categorized into two types based on source: cowhide and pigskin. Cowhide membranes are derived from bovine sources and are often preferred for their strength and structural integrity, making them suitable for various dental and surgical applications. Pigskin membranes, sourced from porcine materials, offer excellent biocompatibility and softness, ideal for enhancing tissue regeneration. Both types are utilized in guided tissue regeneration and periodontal treatments, aiding in healing while being absorbed by the body over time.</p></p>
