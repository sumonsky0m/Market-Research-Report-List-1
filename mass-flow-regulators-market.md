<p><strong>The Mass Flow Regulators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Type</li><li>Indirect Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mass flow regulators market is primarily divided into direct and indirect types. Direct type mass flow regulators provide immediate control of gas or liquid flow by adjusting the flow path based on pressure differences, ensuring precise flow rates. In contrast, indirect type regulators use a downstream feedback mechanism to maintain consistent flow rates, often incorporating additional components for stabilization. Both types are crucial in industries such as pharmaceuticals, chemicals, and aerospace, where accurate flow measurement and control are essential.</p></p>
