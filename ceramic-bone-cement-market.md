<p><p>The ceramic bone cement market comprises three main types based on viscosity: low, medium, and high viscosity cements. Low viscosity cements are fluid, allowing for easy injection and adaptation to complex bone shapes, ideal for minimally invasive surgeries. Medium viscosity cements strike a balance between flowability and stability, suitable for routine orthopedic procedures. High viscosity cements are dense and provide excellent mechanical support, making them suitable for load-bearing applications. Each type caters to specific surgical requirements and patient needs.</p></p>
