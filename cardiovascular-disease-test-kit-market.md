<p><p>The Cardiovascular Disease Test Kit Market can be categorized based on the type of sample used for diagnostic testing. Serum samples are derived from the liquid portion of blood after coagulation, providing key biomarkers for cardiovascular conditions. Plasma samples, which contain clotting factors, are used in tests requiring anticoagulated blood, often yielding more comprehensive results. Whole blood samples include all components of blood, allowing for rapid testing but may require specific handling. Each sample type plays a crucial role in accurate cardiovascular diagnostics.</p></p>
