<p><p>The lithium battery manufacturing machinery market encompasses three primary types: pretreatment, cell assembly, and post-processing. Pretreatment involves preparing raw materials and components, ensuring they meet quality standards. Cell assembly focuses on the integration of cells, where electrodes, separators, and electrolytes are combined to create functional battery cells. Post-processing encompasses finishing touches, including testing, packaging, and quality assurance steps that finalize the battery's readiness for market. Together, these processes ensure the efficient production of high-quality lithium batteries.</p></p>
