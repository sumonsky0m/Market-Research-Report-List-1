<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Mineral Fluid Lubricants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Paraffinic Lubricant</li><li>Naphtenic Lubricant</li><li>Aromatic Lubricant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive mineral fluid lubricants market comprises three primary types: paraffinic, naphthenic, and aromatic lubricants. Paraffinic lubricants are derived from straight-chain hydrocarbons, offering excellent thermal stability and low volatility. Naphthenic lubricants, with their cyclic hydrocarbon structure, provide superior solvency and lower pour points, making them ideal for cold conditions. Aromatic lubricants contain benzene derivatives, known for their high viscosity and excellent lubricating properties, though they are less commonly used due to environmental concerns. Each type serves distinct applications in automotive lubrication.</p></p>
