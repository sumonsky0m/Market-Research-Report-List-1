<p><p>The Non-PVC IV Solution Bags market comprises two primary types: single-chambered and multi-chambered bags. Single-chambered bags contain a single solution for intravenous delivery, simplifying administration and reducing the risk of contamination. In contrast, multi-chambered bags house multiple compartments that can hold distinct solutions, allowing for customized mixtures and reducing the need for multiple infusions. This versatility makes multi-chambered bags ideal for complex treatment regimens, enhancing medication stability and patient safety during intravenous therapy.</p></p>
