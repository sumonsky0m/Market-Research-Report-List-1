<p><p>Dermal infilling materials, primarily used in aesthetic procedures, include hyaluronic acid and calcium hydroxylapatite. Calcium hydroxylapatite, a biocompatible mineral-like compound, offers greater structural support and longer-lasting results, often used for deeper wrinkles and enhancing facial contours. Both materials are key players in the cosmetic dermatology market, catering to diverse patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
