<p><p>The electroplating post-treatment agent market is segmented into three types based on pH levels: acidic, neutral, and alkaline. Acidic agents enhance the adherence and brightness of coatings by adjusting the pH, promoting optimal metal deposition. Neutral agents maintain a balanced pH, ensuring compatibility with various substrates and reducing corrosion risks. Alkaline agents, on the other hand, provide better surface preparation and cleaning, facilitating enhanced metal bonding. Each type serves distinct purposes, catering to specific requirements in electroplating processes.</p></p>
