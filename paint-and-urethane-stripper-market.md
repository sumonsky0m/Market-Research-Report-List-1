<p><p>The paint and urethane stripper market is primarily divided into two types: alkaline and acidic strippers. Alkaline strippers utilize strong bases to break down paint and coatings, making them effective for thick finishes and safe for many surfaces. In contrast, acidic strippers rely on strong acids to effectively dissolve coatings, often working faster but requiring careful handling to prevent damage to surfaces. Both types cater to various user needs, based on the specific application and surface compatibility.</p></p>
