<p><p>The Coronary Artery Disease Treatment Devices Market includes various key types. Coronary stents are used to keep narrowed arteries open post-angioplasty, preventing re-collapse. PTCA (Percutaneous Transluminal Coronary Angioplasty) balloon catheters enlarge blocked arteries by inflating a balloon that compresses plaque against the artery wall. Coronary guidewires facilitate the navigation of catheters through the vascular system, ensuring accurate placement during procedures. Together, these devices play a crucial role in treating coronary artery disease, enhancing blood flow and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
