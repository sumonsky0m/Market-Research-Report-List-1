<p><p>Continuous Compaction Control Systems (CCCS) are vital in construction for real-time monitoring of soil compaction during pavement operations. The market can be divided into two primary types: Single Roller and Double Roller systems. Single Roller systems utilize one roller for compaction, suitable for smaller areas or lighter tasks. In contrast, Double Roller systems employ two rollers to achieve higher efficiency and uniformity in compaction, making them ideal for larger projects requiring enhanced soil stability and performance.</p></p>
