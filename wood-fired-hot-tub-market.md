<p><strong>The Wood-fired Hot Tub Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Heater</li><li>External Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wood-fired hot tub market can be divided into two primary types based on heating methods: internal and external heaters. Internal heaters are built within the tub, using a firebox that directly heats the water, offering a compact design ideal for smaller spaces. In contrast, external heaters are separate units that circulate water through the tub, providing more efficient heating and easier temperature control. Both types cater to different preferences for aesthetics, functionality, and ease of use in outdoor settings.</p></p>
