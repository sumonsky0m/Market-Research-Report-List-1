<p><p>Adhesion barriers are specialized medical products designed to prevent the formation of adhesions, which are fibrous bands that can develop after surgery, potentially leading to complications and chronic pain. The materials used in adhesion barriers can vary, including natural and synthetic substances, which are formulated to be biocompatible and effective in limiting adhesion formation.</p><p>The Adhesion Barriers Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.2% during the forecast period. <p><p>The Adhesion Barriers Market consists of three primary types: film, gel, and liquid. Film barriers are solid sheets that create a physical barrier to prevent tissue adhesion after surgery. Gel barriers are semi-solid materials that can conform to anatomical shapes, providing a flexible protective layer. Liquid barriers are solutions that can be easily applied and spread over a surface, forming a protective film as they dry. Each type serves to minimize postoperative complications arising from tissue adhesion.</p></p>
