<p><strong>The Dry-type Capacitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Filling</li><li>Semi-solid Filling</li><li>Gas Filling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dry-type capacitor market is categorized into three main types based on their filling materials. Solid filling capacitors, using solid dielectrics, offer high reliability and performance, ideal for compact applications. Semi-solid filling capacitors combine both solid and liquid elements, providing a balance of efficiency and thermal management. Gas filling capacitors utilize inert gases to enhance insulation and reduce dielectric losses, making them suitable for high-voltage and high-frequency applications. Each type caters to specific operational requirements and environmental conditions.</p></p>
