<p><strong>The Wafer Aligner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum Aligner</li><li>Atmosphere Aligner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer aligner market is segmented into two main types: vacuum aligners and atmosphere aligners. Vacuum aligners utilize a vacuum system to hold and position wafers with high precision, minimizing contamination and improving alignment accuracy, making them ideal for advanced semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, atmosphere aligners operate in an open environment, suitable for less sensitive applications. While they may offer more flexibility and lower costs, they generally provide lower precision compared to vacuum aligners, catering to different manufacturing needs.</p></p>
