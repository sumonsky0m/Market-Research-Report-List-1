<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Continuous Level Measurement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Differential Pressure</li><li>Guided Wave Radar</li><li>Non-contact Radar</li><li>Ultrasonic</li><li>Capacitance</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuous level measurement technologies include various market types: </p><p>1. **Differential Pressure** sensors measure the pressure difference between two points, indicating liquid levels.</p><p>2. **Guided Wave Radar** uses a probe to measure level based on microwave reflection, suitable for various materials.</p><p>3. **Non-contact Radar** employs microwave signals to gauge levels without physical contact, ideal for challenging conditions.</p><p>4. **Ultrasonic** sensors utilize sound waves to detect levels non-invasively.</p><p>5. **Capacitance** sensors measure changes in capacitance caused by liquid levels.</p><p>6. **Other** methods may include laser or float-based technologies, enhancing versatility in applications.</p></p>
