<p><p>Exterior Insulation and Finish System (EIFS) is a popular energy-efficient cladding system for buildings. It primarily comes in two market types: polymer-based and polymer-modified. Polymer-based EIFS utilizes synthetic materials, providing a lightweight and flexible finish that enhances moisture resistance and aesthetic appeal. Polymer-modified EIFS, on the other hand, combines traditional cement-based solutions with polymers to enhance durability and adhesion, effectively improving impact resistance and weatherproofing capabilities. Both types contribute to enhanced energy efficiency and exterior protection in construction.</p></p>
