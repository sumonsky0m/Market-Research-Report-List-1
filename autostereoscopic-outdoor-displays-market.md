<p><p>The autostereoscopic outdoor displays market includes several key types. Parallax barriers utilize a layer with slits to direct light to each eye, creating a 3D effect without glasses. Integral photography captures 3D images using micro-lenses, allowing multi-directional views. Lenticular arrays consist of lenses that project different images to each eye, enhancing depth perception. Additionally, other market types may include holographic displays and advanced projection techniques, all offering immersive visual experiences ideal for marketing, entertainment, and information dissemination in outdoor settings.</p></p>
