<p><p>The Positive Airway Pressure (PAP) machines market includes several types catering to different respiratory conditions. Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) machines provide a constant airflow to maintain open airways, primarily for sleep apnea treatment. Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure (BiPAP) devices offer varying pressure levels for inhalation and exhalation, beneficial for patients with more complex respiratory issues. Adaptive Servo Ventilation (ASV) adjusts pressure based on real-time breathing patterns, offering tailored support for those with central sleep apnea or other respiratory disorders.</p></p>
