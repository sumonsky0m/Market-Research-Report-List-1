<p><p>The medical packaging paper market is divided into two main types: coated paper and uncoated paper. Coated paper features a smooth, glossy finish, enhancing barrier properties against moisture and contaminants, making it ideal for sterile medical products. In contrast, uncoated paper has a more natural texture, providing breathability and flexibility, often used for less sensitive applications. Both types serve crucial roles in ensuring the safety and integrity of medical supplies while complying with industry standards for packaging materials.</p></p>
