<p><p>The viscosity bone cement market can be categorized into three main types: low viscosity, medium viscosity, and high viscosity bone cement. Low viscosity bone cement is ideal for procedures requiring precise placement, as it flows easily into complex bone structures. Medium viscosity bone cement offers a balance between flow and stability, suitable for standard orthopedic applications. High viscosity bone cement is thicker, providing enhanced strength and stability, often used for load-bearing scenarios or in areas subject to high stress during bone regeneration.</p></p>
