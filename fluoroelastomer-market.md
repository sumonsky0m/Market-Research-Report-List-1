<p><p>The fluoroelastomer market comprises three primary types: fluorocarbon elastomers, fluorosilicone elastomers, and perfluoroelastomers. Fluorocarbon elastomers offer excellent chemical and thermal resistance, making them ideal for automotive and aerospace applications. Fluorosilicone elastomers combine the properties of silicone and fluorocarbon, providing additional resilience against extreme temperatures and fluids. Perfluoroelastomers are highly resistant to aggressive chemicals and extreme environments, making them suitable for specialized industrial applications. Each type serves specific needs within various industries, driven by their unique properties.</p></p>
